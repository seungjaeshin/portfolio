About

I am a multimedia designer focusing on graphic design and effective media/marketing productions. One of my fortes is ideating formless ideas to communicating precise messages in an appropriate form with visuals and design content through storytelling.  

Extensive background knowledge in visual design (ranging from traditional to digital mediums), my work is centered around working 360 degrees concepts across a multitude of media platforms.

Based on my past experience, I love working with projects/companies to create a visual product that continues to get better through research and iterations, imbuing empathy and perspective through multimedia designs.
